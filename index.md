## Hey, I'm João.

iOS Engineer by day, chronic tinkerer by night.

I've spent the last five years building mobile apps, sweating the details on architecture, performance, and the kind of UX that feels obvious in hindsight. When I'm not pushing pixels and protocols on iOS, I'm usually down a rabbit hole: decoding aircraft transponders with an RTL-SDR, poking at network traffic, running way too many containers on my homelab, or finally finishing that one retro game I've been meaning to get back to since 2003.

This site is where I write things down. Tutorials when I've figured something out worth sharing. Deep dives when a topic earns it. The occasional rant about something obscure that I can't stop thinking about.

No posting schedule. No content calendar. Just things I find interesting, written well enough that you might too.

Whether you're a coder, a tinkerer, or just plain curious, there's gonna be something here for you.

João
